How to Play & Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: AI makes random moves - good for beginners
  • Medium: AI tries to win but can be tricked - balanced challenge
  • Hard: AI uses advanced algorithm (minimax) - nearly impossible to beat
  • You play as X, and the AI plays as O
  • Click on any empty cell to make your move
  • The first to get 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
